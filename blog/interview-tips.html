3 Practical Tips That Helped Me Get Interview Calls
📝 Published on 21 March 2026
Loading reads...

Getting interview calls is often harder than preparing for interviews. After applying to hundreds of jobs, I noticed a few practical tricks that actually improved my response rate. Here are 3 tips that really helped.

1️⃣ Use the power of cold emails

Try to get the recruiter’s email ID from friends or colleagues who are currently interviewing in that company. A short and polite email with your resume can sometimes work better than just applying on the portal.

Tip: Keep your email short, clear, and attach your resume in PDF.

2️⃣ Maintain two resumes / two application paths

Keep two versions of your resume with different email IDs.

  • From one email, apply directly to all the jobs shared on Telegram / WhatsApp channels
  • From the other email, try to get a referral and then apply

Even if you don’t get a referral, at least you won’t miss the direct application opportunity.

3️⃣ Add strong keywords to your resume

Certain keywords help in ATS filtering. For example, doing an NPTEL course conducted by IITs allows you to mention “IIT” on your resume, which can sometimes improve shortlisting chances.

Applying using these techniques can increase your chances of getting interview calls 😇

Conclusion

Small changes in the way you apply can make a big difference. Instead of only applying on portals, try referrals, cold emails, and keyword optimization together.
